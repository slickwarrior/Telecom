Why Health Awareness Matters

Knowledge is the first step toward prevention. Understanding diseases like Covid-19, Malaria, Flu, and STDs helps communities take the right precautions. Our goal is to make health information accessible, engaging, and accurate.

Major Diseases

Covid-19

A contagious respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Wear masks, wash hands, and maintain social distance.

Malaria

A mosquito-borne disease caused by parasites. Prevent bites and seek treatment early.

Flu

An infectious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. Get vaccinated annually and practice good hygiene.

STDs

Infections spread through sexual contact. Practice safe sex and get tested regularly.
