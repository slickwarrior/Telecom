Learn More About Health

Detailed information on diseases, prevention tips, and health guidelines.

Covid-19

Covid-19 is a respiratory illness caused by SARS-CoV-2. Common symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Prevention includes vaccination, mask use, and regular hand washing.

Malaria

Malaria is spread by infected mosquitoes. Symptoms include fever, chills, and fatigue. Prevention methods include insecticide-treated nets and anti-malarial drugs.

Flu

The flu is caused by influenza viruses. Symptoms include fever, sore throat, and body aches. Annual flu vaccines are recommended for protection.

STDs

Sexually transmitted diseases are spread through sexual contact. Using condoms and regular testing are key prevention methods.
